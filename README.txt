Richard Woods
AI project 1 basic steering behaviour

Controls:
	Mouse to rotate camera
	WASD to move
	Space to shoot

Enemy tints:
	Dark Blue - Seeking
	Blue - Fleeing
	
	Dark Green - Pursuing
	Green - Avoiding
	
	Dark Red - Approaching
	Red - Shooting
	
	Yellow - Idling
	Dark Yellow - Wandering
	
Gameplay:
	6 Enemies spawn at the beginning with one new enemy every 5 seconds.
	If a bullet hits an enemy they die instantly.
	If a bullet or enemy hits the player they die isntantly and the game resets.
